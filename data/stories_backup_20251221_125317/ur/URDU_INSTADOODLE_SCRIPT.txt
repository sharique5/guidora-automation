Socho, tum ek bohot zaroori interview se chookne wale ho kyunke tumhari gaari start nahi ho rahi.

Maya se milo, ek software developer jo apne ibtedai tees ki dehaai mein hai, ek chalak sheher mein rehti hai. Woh mehnati aur talent se bharpoor hai, magar haal hi mein mehsoos kar rahi hai ke rozmarra ki demands aur kaam ki muqabla bandi mein kho si gayi hai. Project ki deadlines sar pe hain, uska boss din ba din zyada mutalba karta ja raha hai, aur woh soch rahi hai ke shayad yeh job stress ke qabil bhi nahi hai.

Aaj subah khaas hai, jab usay ek namwar startup mein job ke liye aakhri interview dena hai, magar uski gaari start nahi ho rahi. Ghabrahat usay gher leti hai, saans tez ho jata hai, aur shikast ka ehsas mandla raha hota hai.

Maya ka pehla reaction frustration aur mayoosi ka ameez hota hai. Uska dimagh us interview ko miss karne ke natayej sochne lagta hai. Usne is mauqe ke liye bohot mehnat ki hai, soch rahi hoti hai ke shayad yeh uske mojooda job stress se break ka moqa hai. Magar, jab woh apni bejan gaari ke paas khadi hoti hai, uska zehan haal hi mein pesh aaye dusray setbacks ki yaadon mein doob jata hai: pichle haftay toota hua laptop, khoi hui mobile phone, ek qareebi dost ke saath ghalat fehmi. Yeh sab chaos ka ek pattern lagta hai, jo uska vision dhundla kar deta hai aur uski rooh ko udaas bana deta hai.

Jab Maya tow truck ka intezar kar rahi hoti hai, woh apne phone mein beparwahi se scroll karti hai aur ek video pe nazar parhti hai jo zindagi ki barik nishaniyon ko pehchan kar taqat aur rehnumai talash karne ka sabak deti hai. Woh video usay gehraai se chhooti hai. Woh apne haal hi mein pesh aaye "musibaton" par ghor karne lagti hai. Kya yeh sab isharay hain jo usay rukne aur apne raaste par dobara ghor karne ko keh rahe hain?

Jab woh interview ke liye bus mein safar kar rahi hoti hai, Maya apne career ko aage badhane ke is andhe run mein apni sehat aur khushi ka nuqsan sochti hai. Woh mehsoos karti hai ke yeh rukawaten darasal zindagi ke zyada mutawazin pehlu ki taraf ishara hain, usay aahista chalne aur woh kya hai jo waqai mayne rakhta hai, is par dobara ghor karne ka keh rahi hain.

Interview mein dakhil hote hue, Maya ek naye sukoon aur perspective ke saath mehsoos karti hai. Woh sawalon ka jawab diyanatdari se deti hai, sirf apni maharat ka izhar hi nahi karti balke aise kaam ke mahol ki khwahish ka izhar bhi karti hai jo sehat aur takhliq ko ahmiyat deta ho. Usay apne andar ek tabdeel mehsoos hoti hai—aik gehri samajh apni zati aur pesha war zaruraton ki.

Maya ki kahani humein yaad dilati hai ke zindagi ke jo isharay humein deti hai, unhe kholne ke liye khula dil aur dimagh rakhna chahiye, jo aksar musibaton ya rukawaton mein chhupay hotay hain. Ye lamhat, haalaanki pehlay pareshan kun hotay hain, magar woh humein behtar khud shanasi aur lachak ka rasta dikha saktay hain. Agar tumhe kabhi kisi setback ka samna ho, to ek lamha ruk kar gehraai mein dekho. Zindagi tumse kya kehna chahti hai?
